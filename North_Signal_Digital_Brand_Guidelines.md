BRAND SYSTEM: North Signal Colors (use exclusively):

Deep Navy: #0D1B3E
Royal Blue: #1B4F9A
Sky Blue: #5BB3E0
Amber Gold: #F0A500
Muted Blue-Gray: #7EA8D4
White: #FFFFFF

Typography:

Display / Headlines: Fraunces (use for all slide titles, section headers, and pull quotes)
Body / Interface: DM Sans Light (use for all body copy, captions, data labels, navigation, and supporting text)
Fallback if unavailable: Georgia (display), Gill Sans (body)

Tone: Authoritative, measured, Minnesota-proud. Commands a room without raising its voice. Think board presentation at a health plan or Fortune 500 — not a startup pitch. Precision over flash.
Logo treatment: "KOPP DIGITAL GROUP" in DM Sans, all caps, wide tracking, in Sky Blue (#5BB3E0) on dark slides and Deep Navy (#0D1B3E) on light slides. Always preceded by a short horizontal rule in Amber Gold (#F0A500).
Color roles:

#0D1B3E Deep Navy — primary backgrounds, full-bleed authority blocks
#1B4F9A Royal Blue — content panels, card backgrounds, secondary sections
#5BB3E0 Sky Blue — labels, links, supporting text, wordmark on dark
#F0A500 Amber Gold — CTAs, horizontal rules, and star/signal moments only. Never use as a large background.
#7EA8D4 Muted Blue-Gray — body copy on dark backgrounds, secondary descriptors
#FFFFFF White — text on dark, clean slide backgrounds when used as light variant

Design rules:

No gradients anywhere — all fills are flat and solid
No drop shadows or bevel effects on any element
No decorative clipart or stock icons
Bullet points use em dashes (—), not dots
Orange (#F0A500) is strictly reserved for accent and CTA moments — never dominant
Optional decorative element: 8-pointed star outline (North Star / MN flag reference) at 5% opacity, large, partially cropped off the edge of dark slides
